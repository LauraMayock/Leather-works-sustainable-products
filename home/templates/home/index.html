Meet the Tanner

Hi there, I'm Jack Murphy, and Leatherworks is my family-run business, established in 1970. My parents founded the company from our home in Louisburg, Co.Mayo, Ireland. I studied leather works in Cordwainers College London then worked with designer Bill Amberg in Notting Hill, London. Since coming home in 2001, I've been developing new designs and styles in the business.
